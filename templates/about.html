Thorin and Company were a group of thirteen Dwarves, the Wizard Gandalf, and the Hobbit Bilbo Baggins, led by Thorin II Oakenshield in TA 2941. Their purpose was to regain the lost Dwarven kingdom of the Lonely Mountain from the dragon Smaug. The journey began before the Lord Of The Rings story and shows how Middle Earth gained the ring from the clutches of Gollum


The story of Thorin and his army is told in three movies:

The Hobbit: An Unexpected Journey
The Hobbit: The Desolation of Smaug
The Hobbit: The Battle of the Five Armies
